Welcome to Project Athlete
Here you'll find information on:
  • The amount of sleep you produce every single night.
  • An option to set personal goals long-term and short-term.
  • Keep track of Supplements you are taking and how much you are taking at the current time: Protein Powder, Creatine, and BCAA.
  • Daily workouts specific to your sport for example an NFL Wide receiver could use the app to display workouts that revolve around Routes, Speed drills, and Pyrometric.
  • Keep track of daily water intake.
  • Track your daily Nutrition including macro and carbs.
